The Audiobook

A Dragon Called Môr is a lovely collection of mini adventures featuring a lonely little dragon who makes lots of new friends at Tŷ Hafan, a children's hospice nestling on the coast in Wales.

These beautiful six short stories, which I narrated over the course of the summer, show the importance of being kind, empathy towards others and, above all, being loved.

Length: 34 minutes.
Format: MP3 audio.
Language: English.

Supporting

Tŷ Hafan

Tŷ Hafan is one of the leading paediatric palliative care charities in the UK. Based in south Wales, since 1999 they provide care and support to life-limited children, young people and their families, at every step of their difficult journeys.

£10 / £100

Jilly Bebbington is a long-standing volunteer at Tŷ Hafan and has been visiting the hospice for many years, regularly reading to the children. For them she wrote A Dragon Called Môr.

I decided the join their incredible effort by making Môr's wonderful story come to life with this audiobook.
